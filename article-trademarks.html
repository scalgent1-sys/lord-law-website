All about trademarks

A trademark helps customers identify a business, product, or service in the market.

TopicIntellectual propertyForEntrepreneurs, brands, and companies

Your brand is easier to protect when it is planned early.

A trademark may be a name, word, sign, logo, or other mark that distinguishes goods or services. For businesses, it can become one of the most valuable assets because customers connect the mark with trust and quality.

Many business owners spend money on packaging, signage, websites, and ads before checking if the brand can be protected. A basic trademark review helps reduce the risk of using a name that is weak, unavailable, or too close to another business.

What business owners should check

  • Whether the proposed brand name or logo is available.
  • Whether the mark is distinctive enough to protect.
  • Which goods or services should be covered by the application.
  • Whether someone else is already using a confusingly similar mark.

When trademark help is useful

Trademark help is useful when launching a product, building a personal brand, franchising, selling online, or entering a market where copycats may appear.
