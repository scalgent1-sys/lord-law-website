The risks

Every day, entrepreneurs make legal decisions without realizing the risks.

A missed permit, a poorly written contract, an employee dispute, an overlooked tax obligation, or an improperly registered business can quickly become an expensive problem.

The solution

A practical business-law reference written for Filipino entrepreneurs.

The book uses a question-and-answer format so business owners can understand common legal issues without getting lost in legal jargon.

What is inside

Essential laws every entrepreneur should understand.

  • Business registration process
  • Government permits and compliance
  • Corporations, partnerships, and sole proprietorships
  • Labor law and employment issues
  • Taxation and BIR compliance
  • Trademark and intellectual property protection
  • Obligations and contracts
  • SSS, Pag-IBIG, and PhilHealth
  • Common crimes in business operations
  • Barangay Micro-Business Law

Who it is for

Made for people who run, build, or plan to start a business.

The book is not a replacement for legal advice. It is a practical reference that helps readers know what to ask, what to prepare, and when professional guidance may be needed.
