About the firm

Reliable legal guidance for business and property matters.

Founded in 2012, LORD Law combines corporate, business, and real estate experience for clients who need clear legal direction before they move.

The firm has served local and international individuals, companies, businessmen, banks, financial institutions, hospitals, hotels, pharmaceutical companies, energy providers, listed corporations, condominium associations, BPOs, contractors, retailers, wholesalers, and other business clients.

LORD Law handles real estate work from property acquisition and title review to development, taxation, compliance, and disputes. The firm also supports business registration, contracts, corporate housekeeping, labor, taxation, intellectual property, compliance, and court representation.
