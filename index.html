About the firm

Trusted counsel for corporate and property decisions.

Established in 2012, LORD Law built its practice around corporate, business, and real estate work for local and international clients. The firm advises companies, businessmen, institutions, property owners, and individuals across heavily regulated and transaction-heavy matters.

Its real estate practice covers acquisition, title review, development, taxation, compliance, and dispute strategy. Its corporate work supports formation, governance, contracts, retainers, compliance, labor, taxation, intellectual property, and business disputes.

The firm emphasizes prompt legal solutions, direct communication, and long-term client relationships.
